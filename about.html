Our Story

Setting the Standard for Land Surveying in Kenya

Founded in August 2024, Eden Surveys has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted land surveying firms.

Eden Surveyors is a modern Land surveying and geospatial consultancy based in Kenya, founded in August 2024 under Zamocom (k) Limited to provide reliable, accurate and technology-driven surveying services.

The firm was established by qualified surveying professionals with the vision of delivering high-quality cadastral, engineering and mapping solutions to individuals, developers, institutions and government agencies.

Despite being a young firm, Eden Surveyors is committed to accuracy, integrity, efficiency and client satisfaction, using modern surveying technologies such as GNSS, total stations and GIS to deliver dependable results
