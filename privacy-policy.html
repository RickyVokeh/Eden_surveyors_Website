1. Introduction

Eden Surveys ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://edensurveys.co.ke/ (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our Site or use our professional land surveying services.

By using our Site and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the Data Protection Act, 2019 of Kenya and other applicable privacy laws.

3. How We Use Your Information

Eden Surveys uses your personal information for the following purposes:

  • ✓ To provide surveying services and respond to your inquiries
  • ✓ To process payments and manage client accounts
  • ✓ To communicate with you about your projects, appointments, and service updates
  • ✓ To improve our website functionality and user experience
  • ✓ To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including those under the Survey Act (Cap 299) and the Data Protection Act, 2019 of Kenya
  • ✓ For marketing and promotional purposes with your consent

We process personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently, ensuring compliance with the data protection principles outlined in Kenyan law.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal grounds for processing your personal data:

✅

Where you have given explicit consent for specific purposes (e.g., marketing communications).

Contractual Necessity

To fulfill our contractual obligations to you (e.g., providing surveying services).

Legal Obligation

Legitimate Interests

For improving our services and ensuring the security of our operations, where these interests do not override your privacy rights.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We value your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processors and IT service providers, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities (e.g., Land Surveyors Board, Ministry of Lands).
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the security and integrity of our Site, enforce our policies, or defend against legal claims.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission
  • Access Controls to limit who can view your information
  • Secure Servers to store personal information
  • Regular Security Audits to identify and address vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously strive to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by Kenyan law. For example:

  • Survey records and transaction data may be retained for compliance with the Survey Act and financial regulations.
  • Marketing data is retained until you withdraw your consent.

After the retention period, we will securely delete or anonymize your personal data.

8. Your Data Subject Rights

Right to Access

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
