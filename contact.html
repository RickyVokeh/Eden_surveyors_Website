FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to emails within 24 hours and WhatsApp messages within 2 hours during business days.

Do I need an appointment to visit the office?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment for faster service, especially for detailed consultations.

What documents should I bring?

If possible, bring any land documents you have (title deed, previous survey maps, etc.) for better assistance.

Do you offer after-hours support?

For urgent surveying matters, call our emergency line at +254 728 803 726. Standard rates apply.
